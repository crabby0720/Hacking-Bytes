About Hackthon

A bequest being cultivated by the appropriate persons attempts to bring together the minds of programmers, designers, application developers, and newcomers to the field of programming to produce a big hack. You'll have plenty of opportunities to discuss your ideas with industry experts, build a product, try new things, and, most importantly, spend quality time with your friends. One thing that goes without saying about a hackathon like Hacking Bytes is that you will have a great opportunity to network with working professionals and community leaders. If you're a passionate student and adventurer, you won't want to experience FOMO!
